# Product Vision

**Target user:** Developers who need a private, capable workspace for collaborating with AI across general software and DERO-specific projects.

**Core job:** Let users configure providers and safely use AI chat, tools, MCP, project context, and DERO development workflows from one local-first desktop and terminal workspace.

## Principles

1. Keep user data, credentials, and sensitive actions local, explicit, and permission-gated.
2. Make capable AI workflows clear, inspectable, and dependable across desktop and terminal surfaces.
3. Support DERO builders with simulator-first, safety-conscious development assistance.
4. Prefer useful integrations that preserve a fast, approachable developer experience.

## Roadmap themes

- Strengthen reliable provider, tool, MCP, and project-context workflows.
- Improve developer ergonomics across the Electron app and terminal UI.
- Deepen safe, local-first DERO contract and TELA development support.
- Make AI activity, permissions, and generated artifacts easier to understand and control.
